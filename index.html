We apologize to J.D. Salinger for adapting his novel to cinema. Yet, this movie is, we promise, nowhere near being “phony”. In an attempt to achieve our proposal, we have filmed our work from Holden’s perspective, him being only narrated and not shown -perhaps this is because nobody can act as anti-phony as Holden himself-. With the beloved actors Cüneyt Arkın (Old Spencer), Keanu Reeves (Stradlater), and Türkan Şoray (Jane), Emilia Clarke (Phoebe) and the narrator Brian Johnson, our movie is to revolutionize the phony film industry.

Disclaimer: We swear not to remove and add some scenes making the content wander away from the novel’s plot. However, this doesn’t mean that we haven’t completely thrashed the protagonist and reconstructed our own, creating a unique fanfic. This film isn’t what you would call “ordinary” nor is in accordance with its source material, in any kind of sense.
